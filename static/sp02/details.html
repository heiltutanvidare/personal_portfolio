Delivery

Next day gift delivery

All next-day courier delivered shoes are dispatched from our central production facility in Bergen, Norway.

  • Deliveries are normally made between 8am and 8pm. However, during busy periods, these delivery times may differ slightly, and we reserve the right to deliver between 7am and 9pm.
  • If standard delivery is chosen, we cannot guarantee an exact delivery time
  • We do not guarantee to be able to follow delivery instructions.

International delivery

All international deliveries are passed to a relay network who then pass orders onto local shoe shops.

  • We are unable to provide delivery notifications for international orders.
  • We do not guarantee to be able to follow delivery instructions.

Deliveries are normally made between 8am and 6pm by a florist local to the recipient address. However, during peak periods, deliveries may be made until 8pm.
